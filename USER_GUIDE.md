# 📖 CampusCore: Comprehensive User Guide

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to use every portal within the CampusCore ecosystem.

---

## 🏢 1. Company Portal (Super Admin)
**Purpose**: Global management of the entire platform and multiple institutions.
- **Onboarding**: Create new college entries and set their unique institution IDs.
- **Oversight**: View all registered institutions and their active status.
- **Control**: Access global logs and system-wide configurations.

---

## 🏫 2. College Admin Portal
**Purpose**: Institutional setup and data management.
- **Master Data (Excel Upload)**:
    1. Go to the **Excel Upload** tab.
    2. Download the templates.
    3. Upload Faculty, Room, Batch, and Subject lists in bulk.
- **Configuration**:
    1. Set the **Academic Session** (e.g., 2025-26).
    2. Define period timings, working days, and lunch breaks.
- **User Management**: Create and manage HOD, Faculty, and Student accounts.

---

## 🎓 3. HOD Portal (Head of Department)
**Purpose**: Timetable generation and department oversight.
- **Timetable Generation**:
    1. Select a **Batch**.
    2. Map subjects to Faculty and Rooms.
    3. Click **Generate**.
    4. Use the grid to **manually adjust** slots if needed.
- **Attendance Oversight**:
    1. Access the **Attendance** tab.
    2. View the **Institution Attendance Report** to see departmental trends.
- **LMS Management**: Monitor all courses and materials within the department.

---

## 👨‍🏫 4. Faculty Portal
**Purpose**: Classroom management and student interaction.
- **Attendance**:
    1. Select **Mark Attendance**.
    2. Choose Batch and Subject.
    3. Click **Get Student List**.
    4. Mark Present/Absent/Late and **Save**.
- **LMS Operations**:
    1. **Upload Materials**: Add PDFs, Videos, or Links to modules.
    2. **Assignments**: Upload question PDFs and set deadlines.
    3. **Quizzes**: Build interactive quizzes with multiple-choice questions.
    4. **Grading**: Review student submissions, assign marks, and provide feedback.
- **Reports**: View attendance stats for your own classes.

---

## 🎒 5. Student Portal
**Purpose**: Academic participation and personal tracking.
- **Timetable**: Search for your Batch to see your weekly schedule (color-coded).
- **LMS (Classroom)**:
    1. **Content**: View materials uploaded by faculty.
    2. **Assignments**: Download question PDFs and **Submit** your work.
    3. **Quizzes**: Take live quizzes and see your scores instantly.
- **Attendance**: View your personal attendance percentage per subject and overall.
- **Job Portal**: Browse and apply for internship/job opportunities.

---

## 🤖 6. AI Chatbot
**Purpose**: Real-time academic assistance.
- **Queries**: Ask about your timetable ("What is my next class?").
- **Help**: Get help with academic topics or platform navigation.
- **Safety**: The bot automatically disables during active quiz sessions to prevent cheating.
